Bajaj Finserv Limited, a part of Bajaj Holdings & Investments Limited, is a financial services company focused on lending, asset management, wealth management and insurance.
Its subsidiary Bajaj Finance Limited is a Non-Banking Finance Company engaged in consumer finance, SME finance, commercial lending, and wealth management.

Cautionary Notice Regarding Fake Loan offers with lesser interest rates

We understand that some fraudulent people have created fake email IDs as well as false website links identical with Bajaj Finance Limited or Bajaj Finserv Limited. These fake email IDs and websites are being used to offer loans at lower rates of interest, collect advance money, collect customer account details etc. We also note that via these means, they have regrettably defrauded some prospective customers by luring them with false promises.

Please note, Bajaj Finance Limited has a thorough process for reviewing any prospective loan applications and follows all regulatory guidelines in its processes. The company or its representatives will not call for any advance payment of money from any of its customers, prior to sanctioning the loan. Further, prospective customers should also note that Bajaj Finance Limiteds email ID does not contain the word Gmail in any form.
We therefore would advise prospective customers and public at large to exercise utmost care, caution and diligence when responding and accessing these fraudulent emails and websites to protect themselves against the fraud and criminal acts of the perpetrators.

They are also advised to report any incident of defrauding of money as a result of these fraudulent acts and practices to the authorities in their jurisdiction, i.e. the Police and the Telecommunications regulator, including the Cyber Crime Cell. These incidents can also be referred to Bajaj Finance Limited for appropriate action at our end.
