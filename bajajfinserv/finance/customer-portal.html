Features & Benefits

Visit our online Customer Portal to view and transact on your loan account from anywhere, at your convenience.

It has been designed to provide you ease of access to all your loan details and more:

  1. View loan details
  2. Download statement, interest certificates, NOC and welcome letter
  3. Make payment for missed EMI, part payment and foreclosure payments
  4. View and update contact details online
  5. View and download scanned documents
  6. Avail offers specially customized for you
  7. Plan and buy insurance online

How to Access

To access your loan details:

Login to your account using your username/email id/mobile number and password.


You can also access your loan details from your mobile with the mobile App:

  1. Download our mobile app from your GPRS enabled iphone, android or blackberry phone
  2. Or visit www.bajajfinserv.in and
  3. login using your username and password

Username and password has been sent to you on your registered mobile number.
In case you have forgotten your username and password, you can generate the same instantly online by entering your loan account number.
